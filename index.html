01 — About

Bridging theory and practice

I'm a third-year UX Research student with a deep curiosity about how people interact with complex systems. My goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical concepts and practical product decisions through rigorous, mixed-methods research.

My academic foundation in semiotics, cognitive load theory, and human factors informs how I approach real-world research problems. I don't just find usability issues — I uncover the underlying cognitive and behavioral patterns that explain why they occur.

I'm particularly drawn to evaluative research in emerging interfaces — from VR environments to complex information architectures — where understanding human perception and spatial cognition is critical to product success.

03 — Projects

Selected case studies

Improving Learnability and Mitigating Cybersickness in Tactical VR

Conducted a rigorous mixed-methods usability study on AimXR, a complex spatial VR FPS, testing 8 novice users. Identified 4 critical heuristic failures — including Fitts's Law violations, mental model collapse, and vestibular mismatch — and proposed data-driven redesigns to enhance interaction efficiency and reduce cybersickness.

Evaluating Intrusive UI: Measuring Ad Irritation and User Frustration

Designed and executed a mixed-methods survey of 59 frequent online video viewers to quantify the highest drivers of ad frustration — ad length (Mean: 9.02/10) and mid-roll placement — and proposed a "Differential Pricing Mechanism" balancing platform revenue with user satisfaction.

04 — Process

How I work

01

Discover

Understand the problem space through stakeholder interviews, competitive analysis, and literature review. Define research questions and objectives.

02

Plan

Design the research study — choosing methods, recruiting participants, creating discussion guides, and setting up testing environments.

03

Execute

Conduct research sessions with real users. Facilitate interviews, run usability tests, and gather both qualitative and quantitative data.

04

Synthesize

Analyze data through affinity mapping, thematic analysis, and journey mapping. Identify patterns and translate findings into actionable insights.

05

Deliver

Present findings to stakeholders with clear recommendations. Create deliverables like personas, journey maps, and research reports that drive design decisions.
